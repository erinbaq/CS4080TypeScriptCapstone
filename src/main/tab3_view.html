Advisor and Graduation Information







Academic Standing for Undergraduates:

Good Standing

What It Means

Your Cumulative and CPP GPA are 2.0 or above.


At-Risk

What It Means

You have a CPP or Major GPA below a 2.2.

What Will Happen

The At-Risk hold is placed on your account, and the hold will impact your future registration.

Next Steps

In order to remove the hold, you must complete a Blackboard module. Once completed, the hold will be automatically removed in 48 hours.


Academic Probation

What It Means

You have a Cumulative or CPP GPA below 2.0, but it is above the level for Probation with Contract.

What Will Happen

You will get a Probation Hold. This hold is placed on your account and will affect your future registration.

Next Steps

In order to remove the hold, you must complete two steps:

  1. Complete a Blackboard-based module.
  2. Meet with your advisor. After this meeting, the hold will be removed.

Probation with Contract

What It Means

You have a Cumulative or CPP GPA below the following levels based on class standing:

Probation With Contract
Units Completed GPA
Freshman < 30 <1.5
Sophomore 30-59 <1.7
Junior 60-89 <1.85
Senior 90 > <1.95

What Will Happen

The Probation with Contract hold is placed on your account and the hold will impact your future registration.

Next Steps

In order to remove the hold, you must complete three steps:

  1. Complete a Blackboard based module.

  2. Complete a Probation with Contract worksheet provided by your advisor

  3. Finally, meet with your advisor and after the meeting, the PWC hold will be removed.

Source: Academic Standing Page and Colleges & Departments.
